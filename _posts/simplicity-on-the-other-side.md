## The Braun Philosophy

Dieter Rams' ten principles of good design are more relevant today than ever. In a world of digital noise, clarity is a superpower.

### Precision as a Feature

When we build systems at Allocate, we don't just look for "simple." We look for the simplicity that survives the collision with reality.
